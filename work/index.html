Work

I’m a PhD student in Ecology (in biology) at Komarov Botanical Institute RAS and in Forest Phytology at Mendel University in Brno. My work is dedicated to forest ecology, dendrochronology, and wood anatomy.

Further papers and other academic records will appear on ORCID . Also, there is now a Bibliography section below.

Workflow

My workflow heavily relies on:

  • Linux Debian & Fedora
  • TeX , R & R Markdown
  • i3, the tiling wm
  • zathura
  • pandoc
  • rsync
  • make
  • tmux
  • vifm
  • hugo
  • vim
  • git
  • ssh

You are welcome to learn about these minimal and highly customizible tools on the Internet. Also, check out my dotfiles for some of them.
